Activites at Atlantic Trails

Hiking

Altantic Trails Resort has 15 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes The table below lists some of out guided hikes and their departure times from the main office. Signup in the office for any or all of the hikes during your sta at Atlantic Trials. Our guides will point out many interesting features and landmarks during your hike.

Tour Start Time Description Guide Time
6:00 am 2 mile hike on the upper ridge - watch the sun rise Stephanie 2 hours
8:00 am 4 mile hike in the state park. Transportation leaves from main office Roger 3 hours
1:00 pm 6 mile hike over the full trail and the beach head. Some light climbing Stephanie or Heidi 4.5 hours

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use. The table provies a summary of out kayaks. Be sure of your kayaking skills. Check the links to identify your skill level. If you have any questions, please contact Dave in the main office.

Kayak Type Description Skill Level
Sea Long, flat bottom, cargo area for your personal effects Low to moderate
Surf Shorter, more maneuverable than the sea kayak Moderate to high
White Water Very short, rounded ull, very robust. Highly maneuverable Expert

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Atlantic Trails, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise several times a week. The table below lists some of these trips. See the main office for more details.

Tour Start Time Description Commonly Seen Birds Guide
5:30 am Tuesdays Only - September thru November 1.5 mile hike on the upper ridge. Lasts about 1 hour Green Herons,Northern Harriers,Ospreys Stephanie
6:00 am Saturday and Sunday only - March thru June 3 mile hike on the beach. Lasts about 2 hours Cormorants,Canadian Geese,Least Sandpiper Heidi
5:30 am Thursday Only - June thru August 3 mile hike in the State Park. Some light climbing Lesser Yellowlegs,Willet,Black-bellied Plover Stephanie or Heidi
